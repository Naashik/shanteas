Our Products

The Windsor brand epitomizes British Royalty. This exclusive Ceylon tea pays homage to the tea consumed in Sri Lanka during the Colonial era. Its flavors are defined by the richness and vastly unique characteristics that can’t be replicated anywhere in the world.

The Golden Era exemplifies the unique strength and aroma that Ceylon tea offers. These handpicked tea leaves are artistically produced from the finest facilities in the verdant hills of Ceylon. The Ceylon tea comes with a refined and superior aroma, which epitomizes the natural and traditional flavors of Sri Lanka.

Ceylon is a small island with no distinguishable seasons. However, the island experiences fluctuations in weather patterns following the elevation of the locality. As a result, the tea grown in distinct locations yields a divergent range of aromas, flavors, and properties.

Types Of Teas

Black Tea

This tea type represents the iconic body, strength, color, and pungency of the fine Sri Lankan tea. To get the best out of our black Ceylon tea, brew it for at least three minutes in spring water. Tea enthusiasts that enjoy extra-strong flavors can brew it for 4-5 minutes and complement it with warm, dairy milk- if needed.

Flavored Tea

This blend is recognized for its gentle sweetness, tangy notes, and mild fragrance, making its consumption a delightful experience.

Green Tea

Unfermented Green Ceylon tea. This blend yields a mildly astringent, refreshing taste. It is complemented with a hint of light smoky brew and fresh herbal aromas.

Herbal Tea

Herbal blends include pure, natural, and caffeine-free infusions that amalgamate fruit-based, herbal, and spicy ingredients. Herbal tea yields aromatic, soothing, and indulgent combinations.

Organic Tea

Organic tea is grown on plantations that do not use chemical fertilizers in their growth and processing. The combination includes organic pure tea and natural organic herbal infusions that are IMO certified. The organic range is inclusive of black and green tea.

Multi-Origin Tea

In addition to traditional Ceylon tea, blends of teas with Kenyan, Assam, and Darjeeling (from India) origins.

Wellness Teas

A range of wellness teas, including peace, virility, slimming, detox, and night teas. The wellness tea blends are curated using exclusive herbs of spices and flowers.

Regional Teas

An assortment of regional teas is grown in the various regions of Sri Lanka. Ceylon has seven agro-climatic regions, each producing its unique flavors and variations of traditional Ceylon tea. The regional teas are grown in:

  • Low County - In regions of Ruhuna and Sabaragamuwa
  • Mid Country - In the Kandy region
  • Hill Country - In regions of Nuwara Eliya, Dambulla, Uva & Uda Pussellawa

Silver Tips

Silver tips (White tea) is one of the rarest and most refined forms of tea for discerning tea connoisseurs. This type of tea is created from the hand-picked buds of the Camellia Sinensis plant. The manufacturing process for this tea is also quite different. The buds are hand-picked and sun-dried using a meticulous approach.

Golden Tips

Golden tips are also refined teas that speak to the taste buds of all tea connoisseurs. They are also extracted and handpicked from the Camellia Sinensis plant. The manufacturing process is quite distinct. The buds are handpicked, sprayed with black tea liquid, and sun-dried.

Tisanes

Natural, caffeine-free Tisanes blends. This blend combines fruity, herbal, and spicy flavors to deliver an aromatic, relaxing, and indulgent infusion.

Fruit Teas

Pure Ceylon tea with natural fruit bits and hints of fruity flavors. Teas deliver the taste of actual fruit in a cup to tea consumers.
