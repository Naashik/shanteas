Windsor

The Windsor brand epitomizes British Royalty. This exclusive Ceylon tea pays homage to the tea consumed in Sri Lanka during the Colonial era. Its flavors are defined by the richness and vastly unique characteristics that can’t be replicated anywhere in the world.


Our Windsor brand deeply considers the royal nature of Ceylon tea. It has a rich, aromatic, and flavorful blend that offers opulence and luxuriousness.
The Windsor range includes high-end teas and blends that perfectly complement the Sri Lankan tea culture's purity, quality, and freshness. Consuming the Windsor brand makes you feel like royalty.
